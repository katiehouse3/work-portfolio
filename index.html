Welcome to my portfolio! I am a Data Scientist at MassMutual with experience building end-to-end machine learning solutions in generative AI, marketing funnel optimization, advisor success, market mix modeling (MMM), and leads in the Marketing and Distribution domain. I have my M.S. in Computer Science from UMass Amherst, concentrating in Data Science, and my undergraduate degree in Industrial Engineering from Northeastern University. Besides working, I spend my time baking, pouring latte art, and having quality time with my family (including a 90-pound Bernese Mountain Dog!) I was also the founder of HackHer413, a hackathon that fosters inclusivity and diversity in tech at UMass Amherst.
